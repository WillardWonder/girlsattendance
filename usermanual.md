Goal: Check in for practice in under 10 seconds.

Open the App: Scan the QR code or go to the team link on the iPad/Tablet.

Find Your Name:

Tap the search bar.

Type the first few letters of your last name (e.g., "Smi" for Smith).

Select your name from the list.

Enter Data:

Weight: Enter your current weight (e.g., 145.5).

Skin Check: Tap Yes if you have passed your skin check. Tap No if you have a concern.

Finish:

Tap the blue "Check In" button.

Wait for the green "Success" screen.

You are done! The screen will reset automatically for the next teammate.

ðŸ“‹ For Coaches: Admin Dashboard Guide

How to Access:

On the main check-in screen, scroll to the very bottom.

Click "Coach Dashboard".

Enter the team password: bluejays

Tab 1: Live Practice (Daily Management)

Use this tab during practice to see who is in the room.

Stats Cards: See a quick count of Present vs. Absent athletes.

Checked In List:

Shows everyone currently at practice with their weight and check-in time.

Skin Issues: Any athlete who marked "No" on skin check will have a red alert next to their name.

Mistake? If a student checked in by accident, tap the X / Trash Icon on the right side of their name to remove them from today's list.

Absent List: Scroll down to see exactly who hasn't arrived yet.

Tab 2: History & Reports (Attendance Records)

Use this tab for AD reports, lettering requirements, or weekly reviews.

Recent Activity: Shows a quick list of past practices and total attendance counts.

Report Builder:

Select a Start Date and End Date.

(Optional) Filter by a specific Athlete to see their attendance record.

(Optional) Filter by Grade (e.g., just show Seniors).

Click "Download Report (CSV)" to save an Excel-ready file of all matching records.

Tab 3: Roster Tools (Team Management)

Load Full Team: If you wiped the database, click the blue "Load Full Team Roster" button to instantly add the standard roster (39 athletes) back into the system. It automatically checks for duplicates before adding.

Maintenance:

Fix Duplicate Entries: If you see "John Smith" twice, click this button. It scans for matching names+grades and deletes the extras automatically.

Quick Add: Use the input box at the bottom to add a single new wrestler (Lastname, Firstname).

Danger Zone: The red "Delete Entire Roster" button wipes the list of names. It does not delete past attendance history, but it requires you to reload the roster names to allow check-ins again.
